I always wished that people could make faces like the ones in cartoons or anime.

Then, when a person is angry, they could contort their face

or if they were embarrassed, they would blush, their eyebrows upturned.

I can't tell what I am making people feel.

Are they angry at me? Or too shy to speak? Are they simply focused?

People who smile all the time frighten me.

I was taught in school that people smile when they're happy. So why do smiling faces keep confusing me?

How can I know that you're angry if you don't shout, or sad if you're not hushed?

How can I know if you want me to go away?
