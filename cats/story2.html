My family needs me to be smart, confident, and reliable.

My mother is a first-generation immigrant. She works at a call center.

When she found out I had been diagnosed with clinical depression, she was angry.

I think it was because she had spent her whole life working so that I could have a better life than she did

just to find out that really, I had been struggling the whole time.

I tried not to mention that I was depressed after that.

My sister has a lot of needs.

She has trouble finishing assignments, talking, eating in public, eating new things, doing new things, taking the bus, controlling her anger, and forgiving others.

My parents used to tell me about how when I was born, the doctors were shocked because I didn't cry.

My sister was the opposite. She was a colicky baby, so they spent a lot of time keeping her calm.

I love my sister. She cries louder than me.

I am smart, confident, and reliable.
