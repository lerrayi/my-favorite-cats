When people assure me that I am normal, it makes me angry.

If everyone feels like me, then why would they make the world so difficult to navigate?

It feels impossible just to muster the strength to smile during conversations.

How could I possibly make myself look acceptable to you?

Still, I try to smile more, and I talk to myself in the car, and make faces in the mirror.

Why does it feel so unnatural to do things this way?

I wish I could code myself to respond to actions, the same way a robot can.

Metal would be much better than this body

that keeps sabotaging itself.
